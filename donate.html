Support the Mission

Your support helps Community Angels serve seniors, families, and neighborhoods throughout the community.

Why Donations Matter

Donations help Without Helping Hands purchase cleaning supplies, yard equipment, safety gear, transportation support, and other materials needed to complete community service projects.

Every contribution helps us reach more seniors, assist more families, create opportunities, and improve more neighborhoods.

Cleaning Supplies

Help provide trash bags, gloves, disinfectants, brooms, buckets, and other cleanup materials.

Yard Equipment

Help provide rakes, lawn equipment, leaf bags, tools, and protective gear for outdoor projects.

Senior Support

Help with transportation, errands, household needs, and other support for older community members.

Community Jobs

Help us create paid opportunities and training for people who want to serve and improve their neighborhoods.
