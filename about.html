About Without Helping Hands

Connecting Community Angels back with the community.

Who We Are

Without Helping Hands is a community support app created to connect people who need assistance with Community Angels who are ready to help.

We believe stronger neighborhoods are built when people work together, support seniors, create opportunities, and take care of the places they call home.

Why We Were Created

Too many seniors, families, and community members need help with everyday responsibilities but do not know where to find dependable support.

At the same time, many people are searching for meaningful work, volunteer opportunities, and ways to serve their communities.

Without Helping Hands brings both sides together in one trusted app.

What We Do

Our Community Angels help connect people with services such as neighborhood cleanup, yard work, senior assistance, household help, and other community needs.

We also work to create local jobs, provide volunteer opportunities, improve neighborhoods, reduce pests and unsafe conditions, and rebuild stronger community relationships.

Our Vision

We are starting in Washington, D.C., Maryland, and Virginia, with a long-term vision of helping communities everywhere.

Our goal is to build an app where residents can request help, Community Angels can find opportunities, and neighborhoods can work together to become cleaner, safer, and stronger.

Become Part of the Mission

Whether you need assistance, want to volunteer, or are looking for an opportunity to work and serve, there is a place for you in Without Helping Hands.
