The Story Behind Without Helping Hands

In 2018, Randolph Jarmon wrote down a vision for his future while facing difficult circumstances.

He believed that determination, persistence, faith, and purpose could build something bigger than himself.

Years later, that vision began to take form when he started cleaning his community streets alone, wearing a jacket he designed himself with the words "Without Helping Hands."

What began as one person helping his neighborhood grew into the idea for a platform where people can work, help others, and restore their communities together.

That experience showed mesomething important - many people want to help their communities, but they don't always have a system that connects effort, opportunity, and purpose.
